## Topic arc

### Day 1 — Difference-in-Differences (foundations → modern)
- Potential outcomes; canonical 2×2 DiD; parallel trends and pre-testing
- Goodman-Bacon decomposition; problems with TWFE under staggered adoption
- Callaway-Sant'Anna; Sun-Abraham; Borusyak-Jaravel-Spiess
- Sensitivity (Roth-Sant'Anna; Rambachan-Roth)
- Hands-on Stata/R lab

### Day 2 — Continuous DiD + Synthetic Control
- Continuous-treatment DiD: identification, estimation, recent literature
- Synthetic control: ADH; permutation + conformal inference
- Augmented and penalized SCM (Ben-Michael-Feller-Rothstein)
- Synthetic difference-in-differences (Arkhangelsky et al.)
- Hands-on Stata/R lab; choosing methods in practice
